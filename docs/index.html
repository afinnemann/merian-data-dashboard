What this is

A curated catalogue of openly accessible environmental and health datasets for São Paulo and the Netherlands.

Built as a prototype for the five NWO/FAPESP Merian "Extreme Heat and Water Events" consortia (2026–2031), to support shared dataset discovery across the partner teams.

Use the cards to locate authoritative sources, compare temporal and spatial resolution, period of coverage, and licence at a glance, and trace which environmental datasets can be joined to health-outcome anchors. Filter by country or domain to narrow the view. Each card also includes a short R snippet for fetching a small illustrative slice.
