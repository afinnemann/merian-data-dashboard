# Merian Environmental Data — Starter set v0.1
# Conjunto inicial de Dados Ambientais Merian — v0.1

**EN —** A prototype catalogue of seven openly-accessible environmental and health datasets for São Paulo and the Netherlands, with an R snippet per dataset (`head()` preview only). Built for discussion across the five NWO/FAPESP Merian "Extreme Heat and Water Events" consortia. Framing, source list and metadata fields will be revised after consortium consultation.

**PT-BR —** Catálogo-protótipo com sete conjuntos de dados ambientais e de saúde de acesso aberto para São Paulo e Holanda, com um trecho em R por conjunto (apenas pré-visualização via `head()`). Construído para discussão entre os cinco consórcios NWO/FAPESP Merian "Eventos Extremos de Calor e Água". O enquadramento, a lista de fontes e os campos de metadados serão revisados após a consulta entre os consórcios.

---

## Brazil / Brasil

- **clh-br-0001 · CETESB QUALAR** — São Paulo air-quality network (PM, NO₂, O₃, SO₂, CO), hourly. *LicenseRef-CETESB · free registration.* https://qualar.cetesb.sp.gov.br
- **clh-br-0002 · BR-DWGD (Xavier et al.)** — Gridded daily T and precip, ~10 km, 1961–present. *CC-BY-4.0 · doi:10.1002/joc.7012.* https://sites.ufpe.br/lampe/br-dwgd/
- **clh-br-0003 · CHIRPS rainfall** — Satellite + gauge daily precipitation, ~5 km, 1981–present. *CC0-1.0 · Funk et al. 2015.* https://www.chc.ucsb.edu/data/chirps
- **clh-br-0007 · DATASUS SIH** — SUS hospital admissions (AIH) by ICD-10, municipality, month, 1992–present. *LicenseRef-DATASUS · Ministério da Saúde.* https://datasus.saude.gov.br/transferencia-de-arquivos/

## Netherlands / Holanda

- **clh-nl-0001 · RIVM Luchtmeetnet** — National air-quality network, hourly, open API. *CC-BY-4.0 · RIVM.* https://www.luchtmeetnet.nl/open-data
- **clh-nl-0002 · KNMI Daggegevens** — Daily station meteorology (T, precip, humidity, wind), 1901–present. *CC-BY-4.0 · KNMI.* https://www.knmi.nl/nederland-nu/klimatologie/daggegevens
- **clh-nl-0007 · CBS Doodsoorzakenstatistiek** — National cause-of-death statistics by ICD-10, table 70895NED. *CC-BY-4.0 · CBS StatLine.* https://opendata.cbs.nl/statline/#/CBS/nl/dataset/70895NED/table
